NameTotalErrorRateEvaluationMeasure


FormulaTBD


DescriptionMeasures the total overlapping pixels in two images (a reference image and an estimated one) divided by the total pixels in the estimated image. Gives a measure of the degree to which both images correspond to the estimated image.


CategoryVoxel


Referencehttp://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/cyto.a.21079/pdf


Adapter(s)DicomImageObjectAdapter


Extractor(s)VoxelToArrayFeatureExtractor


Descriptor(s)VoxelToArrayFeature


Note(1)As a cautionary note, if pixel measures are run on very large files or color images, they may take a large amount of memory, computing time, or both.
